Medical Services

These are the services doctors accessible through Check App

Family Physician

A medical practitioner trained to care for a person from infancy to advanced age.

Pediatrician

A medical practitioner specializing in children and their diseases.

Gynecologist

A medical practitioner who specializes in female reproductive health.

Cardiologist

A medical practitioner who specializes in the study or treatment of heart diseases and heart abnormalities

Oncologist

A medical practitioner qualified to diagnose and treat tumours.





Gastro - enterologist

Doctors who specialize in the digestive system, from the mouth to the anus, and the liver.

Pulmonologist

A medical doctor who specializes in the respiratory system.

Nephrologist

A medical practitioner that deals with the physiology and diseases of the kidneys.

Ophthalmologist

A specialist in the branch of medicine concerned with the study and treatment of disorders and diseases of the eye.

Optician

a person qualified to prescribe and dispense glasses and contact lenses, and to detect eye diseases (ophthalmic optician) or to make and supply glasses and contact lenses (dispensing optician).





Otolaryngologist

A doctor that specializes in treating conditions that affect the ears, nose, and throat, as well as head and neck surgery.

Physio - therapist

A person qualified to treat disease, injury, or deformity by physical methods such as massage, heat treatment, and exercise.

Dermatologist

A medical practitioner specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of skin disorders

Psychiatrist

A medical practitioner specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of mental illness.

Neurologist

A medical specialist in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system

Read More




Dentist

A person qualified to treat diseases and other conditions that affect the teeth and gums, especially the repair and extraction of teeth and the insertion of artificial ones

Geriatrician

An expert in the branch of medicine or social science dealing with the health and care of old people

Hematologists

A doctor or pediatrician who have extra training in disorders related to your blood, bone marrow, and lymphatic system.

Endocrinologist

A doctor who specialize in hormonal issues and the glands that produce these hormones. A diabetes specialist.

Sports physician

A health care provider who treats athletes and active people who want to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
